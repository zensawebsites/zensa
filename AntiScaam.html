Anti-Scam Policy

Stay safe on Zensa • Updated November 2025

Your Safety Comes First

Zensa is committed to protecting users from fraud, scams, and harmful activities. Please read these guidelines carefully to stay safe when buying or selling services on our platform.

1. Common Scam Techniques

Be aware of these common scam methods:

  • Users asking for advance payments before providing any proof of work
  • Fake profiles using stolen photos or false information
  • Suspicious links or files sent through chat
  • Unrealistic offers or extremely low-priced services
  • Requests to move conversation outside Zensa

2. How to Protect Yourself

Follow these simple steps to stay secure:

  • Avoid paying anyone without verification
  • Never share personal information or passwords
  • Do not click unknown links sent by users
  • Check reviews, profiles, and service history before dealing
  • Use Zensa chat to communicate—avoid external messaging apps

3. Signs of a Fake or Suspicious User

  • No profile photo or incomplete information
  • Always rushing you to pay quickly
  • Unprofessional behavior or inconsistent answers
  • Not willing to provide any verification of identity or work
  • Making unrealistic promises (e.g., “100% guarantee in 1 hour”)

4. What to Avoid Doing

  • Do not send advance payments to unknown users
  • Do not share your login, OTP, or banking details
  • Do not accept suspicious files or download unknown attachments
  • Do not continue any deal if you feel uncomfortable or unsafe

5. What Zensa Does to Protect You

We actively monitor the platform and take action against scammers by:

  • Blocking accounts involved in suspicious activity
  • Removing harmful or fraudulent ads
  • Verifying user information when necessary
  • Responding quickly to reports and complaints

6. How to Report a Scam

If you encounter suspicious activity:

  • Click “Report User” on their profile or ad
  • Contact Zensa support with screenshots and details
  • Stop communication with the suspicious user immediately

7. Our Zero-Tolerance Policy

Zensa takes scam-related behavior very seriously. Any user involved in fraud, deception, or harmful activities will be permanently removed from the platform.

Stay Safe, Stay Smart

Your safety is our top priority. If something feels suspicious, trust your instincts and report it immediately. Zensa is here to keep your experience safe and secure.
