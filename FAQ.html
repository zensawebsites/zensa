Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Updated November 2025 • Find clear answers to common questions.

Welcome to the Zensa FAQ Page

Here you’ll find answers to common questions about using Zensa. If you still need help, our support team is always available.

1. What is Zensa?

Zensa is an online marketplace where users in Pakistan can buy and sell services. Whether you’re a skilled professional or someone needing assistance, Zensa helps you connect safely and quickly.

2. How do I create an account?

  • Click the “Sign Up” button
  • Enter your basic details
  • Verify your phone number or email
  • Start posting services or browsing ads

Make sure the information you provide is correct, as fake accounts are not allowed.

3. How do I post my service?

  • Log into your account
  • Click “Post Service”
  • Add clear photos, descriptions, and pricing
  • Submit for review

Your service will appear once approved. Only real, honest, and clear ads are accepted.

4. Is posting a service free?

Yes, posting a service on Zensa is free. We may introduce optional premium features later.

5. How do buyers contact me?

  • Through the chat option on your service page
  • Your shared contact details (if allowed)

Never share your password or private information with anyone.

6. Does Zensa handle payments?

No. Payments are made directly between buyers and sellers. Always pay trusted users only.

7. How can I stay safe on Zensa?

  • Avoid sending money to unverified users
  • Check seller information carefully
  • Report suspicious users immediately
  • Do not upload illegal or harmful content

8. What type of services are not allowed?

  • Illegal or harmful services
  • Adult content
  • Fraudulent or misleading listings
  • Spam or abusive behavior
  • Selling stolen items
  • Uploading malicious files

9. Why was my ad removed?

  • False or misleading information
  • Violating Zensa rules
  • Suspicious or harmful behavior
  • Scam-related activity

Our team actively works to keep Zensa safe for everyone.

10. Can my account be blocked?

  • Using a fake identity
  • Repeated rule violations
  • Scamming or fraudulent behavior
  • Posting harmful content
  • Misusing the platform

You may contact support if you believe your account was blocked by mistake.

11. How do I report a problem or user?

  • Click the “Report” button on their profile or ad
  • Contact our support team
  • Send complete details through the help center

12. How can I contact Zensa support?

  • Visit the “Support” section on the website
  • Use our contact form or email
  • Use the help-center chat

We aim to respond as quickly as possible.
