Privacy Policy

How we protect your data • Updated November 2025

1. Introduction

At Zensa, your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account details (name, email, phone number)
  • Uploaded content (ads, images, descriptions)
  • Device and browser information
  • Location data (if permitted)
  • Payment-related information when using paid services

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Verify accounts and prevent scams
  • Customize your user experience
  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Process payments securely

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only with:

  • Trusted service providers (payment gateways, analytics tools)
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Third parties only with your consent

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our platform. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Data Security

We use advanced security measures to protect your account and personal information from unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Delete your account
  • Opt out of marketing communications

8. Children's Safety

Zensa is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the platform means you accept the new changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests related to privacy, contact us at support@zensa.com.
